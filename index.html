How does our platform work?

  • Step 1.

    Buy an SIT

    Purchase SIT on a decentralised crypto-exchange in order to use them on the Sport Investing platform.

  • Step 2.

    Choose a promising player or a club for investment

    Register and choose an athlete or a club or consult experienced analysts on our analytical portal when choosing a token.

  • Step 3.

    Choose a promising player or a club for investment

    Invest in the token assigned to an athlete or a club with SIT according to the current market price based on a publicly available source.

  • Step 4.

    Track the value of the purchased tokenised assets

    Monitor the change in the market price of the tokenised athlete or club that you have purchased on the Sport Investing platform and receive an email notification when the price starts to rise sharply.

  • Step 5.

    Sell the tokens

    When the market value of the acquired tokenised assets assigned to a player or a club rises, you can sell them and receive SIT tokens at a current cost.

  • Step 6.

    Get a profit

    You can exchange SIT tokens for any other cryptocurrency on the market or purchase new tokenised shares assigned to other players or clubs on the platform

What is an SIT?

In order to buy athletes'/clubs' tokens on the platform, users deal with SIT, which can be traded on crypto-exchanges.

The token is launched on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP20) and has already been verified by the Binance Smart Chain Explorer validator.

In the third quarter of 2021 the token is to become multi-chain and is going to be available on such blockchains as Freeton, Ethereum and Tron. It is also going to feature our own product "SIT connecting bridge".
